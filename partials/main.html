HELLO, PLAYER!

I welcome you to play a simple card game.
There is a deck of cards where each card has a pair (there are at least 2x of each card in the deck). Cards are laid out face-down in a grid in random order. You can flip two cards at a time. If flipped card faces match, they stay face up. If cards don't match, they are placed face down again.
You win when all cards are facing up!
